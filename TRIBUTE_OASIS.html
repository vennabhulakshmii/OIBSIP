Azim Premji

INTRODUCTION:

Azim Hashim Premji is an Indian business tycoon, investor, engineer, and philanthropist, who is the chairman of Wipro Limited. He is informally known as the Czar of the Indian IT Industry. Under his leadership, Wipro has become one of the leading IT companies in India.

ACHIEVEMENTS:

Apart from his business achievements, Premji is also known for his philanthropic work. He has donated a significant portion of his wealth to charity through the Azim Premji Foundation, which focuses on education in India.

Azim Premji's contributions to the IT industry and his philanthropic endeavors have earned him numerous accolades and recognition both in India and internationally.
